The (Unofficial) Internet Archive Fundraising Zine!

What's This Zine?

The Internet Archive is an amazing repository of old websites, books, zines, music, games, and more, available to the public for free! In these times their work to preserve our culture and history is more important than ever, and they need our support.

In honor of International Archives Week 2025, a group of zinesters, webweavers, and archive fans got together to run a fundraiser for the Internet Archive. For every donation of $5 or more we found a cool thing in the Internet Archive; donors could request a topic, or we chose a wildcard. And at the end we collected all of them into this free zine!

Over the course of the week we received {{researchItems.length}} donations totalling ${{total}}! Thank you so so much to everyone who donated. If you missed your chance and want to donate, you can do so directly at archive.org/donate. Let's help keep the Internet Archive alive!
