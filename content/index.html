The (Unofficial) Internet Archive Fundraising Zine!

Intro

The Internet Archive is an amazing repository of old websites, books, zines, music, games, and more, available to the public for free! In these times their work to preserve our culture and history is more important than ever, and they need our support.

In honor of International Archives Week 2025, a group of zinesters, webweavers, and archive fans got together to run a fundraiser for the Internet Archive. For every donation of $5 or more, we found a cool thing that lives in the Internet Archive, with the option for donors to request a topic. And then we collected them into this zine!

If you donated to our fundraiser, thank you so much! And if you'd like to donate to them directly, you can do so at archive.org/donate.
